### Short Biography

Yan Zhao is an assistant professor with Aalborg University. She received the Ph.D. (in 2020) degree of Computer Science from Soochow University (advised by [Prof. Kai Zheng](http://zheng-kai.com/)) and received the Master (in 2015) degree of Geographic Information System from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. Her research interests include spatial database, trajectory analysis, data mining, machine learning, and deep learning.
